Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is the CEEQ?

CEEQ - Comprehensive End-to-End Quality is the novel framework to describe the measurement of IVR Applications based on tracked issues in JIRA system.

Q: Why I cannot look into the quality score of IVR Apps?

You need log in with your West Credential.

Q: Why I cannot log in with West Credential?

Please access West Identity Manger to check your username and password, or contact EIT 800-838-8383 for further support.

Q: What kind of browsers are supported?

The following browsers have validated for accessing this site:

  • Internet Explorer 9+
  • Google Chrome 29+
  • Mozilla Firefox 24+

Q: What the score's representation?

CEEQ ranges from 0 -10 to illustrate the quality of IVR Apps. CEEQ helps our testing procedure on assembled IVR Apps become more quick, easy and accurate through software lifecycle.

Q: Why I cannot edit the project information?

Only super user has authority to edit project information. General user has the authority to access the quality score of the project.

Q: What's the role of JIRA issue's priority?

We take different priority of issues as different weighted factor to our framework.

Q: Why we need Testing Characteristics?

Software Testing Characteristics describe the IVR Applications testing procedure during it's software lifecycle. It will affect quality score of project in some degree.
